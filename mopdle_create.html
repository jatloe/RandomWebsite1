Guidelines:

The hints should generally be specific enough. You want the player's reaction when seeing the first clue to be "what the heck is that" instead of, say, "bro that applies to like half the moppers".

You should maintain a very visible difficulty curve. A person who does not know it is you, upon seeing the clues, should be able to sort them in the order you did without much trouble.

In particular, make the last two clues easy enough that the proportion of X/5's is less than 10%.
